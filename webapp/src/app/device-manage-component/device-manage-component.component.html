You can write new playlists to your device here or update your room.

Write a tag

Enter the playlist you want to play with the nfc chip. Then press the magic ward to active tagging mode.

Your device will light up red to indicate write mode. The next tag presented will be written to activate the playlist.

After writing the playlist the device will light up blue.

Configuration

Update the configuration of your Device. If your device is online, your current configuration should be loaded automatically.
